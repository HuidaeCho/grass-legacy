Historical Notes

GRASS releases

    • 1982 Fort Hood Information System (FHIS) (Vax 11/780)
    • 1983 Installation Geographic Information System (IGIS)  (SUN-1 Microcomputer)
    • 1984 GRASS (SUN-1 and Masscomp)
    • 1985 GRASS 1.0, GRASSnet established
    • 1986 GRASS 1.1;  receives "Exemplary Systems in Government Award" from URISA
    • 1987 GRASS 2.0; first issue of GRASSClippings, GRASS video narrated by William Shatner (Captain Kirk of Star Trek)
    • 1988 GRASS 3.0; Army R&D Achievement Award (Webster, Goran, Shapiro, Westervelt)
    • 1991 GRASS 4.0; released through Internet
    • 1993 GRASS 4.1; Federal Lab Consortium - Award for Excellence in Technology Transfer
    • 1995 GRASS 4.1.5 port to Linux: Yung-Tsung Kang, Michigan State University
    • 1997 GRASS4.2 Baylor University
    • 1998 GRASS4.2.1 Markus Neteler, Germany
    • 1999 GRASS5.0 Baylor University and Markus Neteler
